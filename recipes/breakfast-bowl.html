Breakfast Bowl

Description

Fuel your morning with this surprisingly flavorful bowl of goodness! Packed with protein and good-for-you ingredients like egg, red quinoa, creamy avocado, and tangy feta, it's a breeze to throw together.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup red quinoa
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Stir water and quinoa together in a rice cooker; cook until quinoa is tender, about 15 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook eggs to desired doneness; season with seasoned salt and pepper.
  3. Combine quinoa and eggs in a bowl; top with avocado and feta cheese.
