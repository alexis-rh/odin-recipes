French Toast

Description

A yummy baked French Toast to start your day off! This recipe is always a hit and so easy to make. You can customize it by adding your favorite fruit or nuts for toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread, cut diagonally in 1 inch slices
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cups butter
  • 1 1/3 cups brown sugar
  • 3 tablesspoons light corn syrup

Instructions

  1. Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish; arrange slices of bread on the bottom.
  2. Beat eggs, milk, cream, vanilla, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Pour over bread slices, cover, and refridgerate overnight.
  3. The next morning, preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Meanwhile, combine butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup together i na small saucepan; heat until bubbling.
  4. Pour over bread and egg mixture.
  5. Bake in preheated oven, uncovered, for 40 minutes.
  6. Serve with berries or nuts and syrup.
